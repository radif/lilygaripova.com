Downtown BART корридор кондо, Rossmoor 55+ community, Northgate и Lime Ridge single-family инвентарь, three-district school split (WCSD K-8 + Acalanes UHSD 9-12 ИЛИ Mt Diablo Unified K-12), John Muir Walnut Creek Level III NICU.

Fremont, CA

Mission San Jose / Warm Springs vs Centerville / Niles / Irvington vs Ardenwood / Glenmoor, три отдельных sub-рынка. Apple-Tesla commuter pools, top-California-tier Mission San Jose High, две BART-станции.

Castro Valley, CA

Тихий unincorporated East Bay community, top-rated Castro Valley Unified schools (часто выше Hayward Unified). Hillside инвентарь (Five Canyons, Greenridge) vs valley-floor SFH вокруг BART. BART access, редкость для unincorporated East Bay.

San Ramon, CA

Северный Tri-Valley sister city Dublin / Danville. San Ramon Valley Unified (top California). Windemere + Gale Ranch master-planned (пост-2000, высокий Mello-Roos). Bishop Ranch employer anchor (Chevron, AT&T, PG&E, GE).

Livermore, CA

Восточно-конечный Tri-Valley город. Wine country lifestyle (Livermore Valley AVA, 50+ виноделен 15-25 мин east). LLNL / Sandia employer cluster. 15-25% price discount к Pleasanton для сопоставимого инвентаря; нет BART (terminus Dublin/Pleasanton, 10-15 мин drive).

Single city-county. SFUSD использует choice-enrollment (адрес НЕ гарантирует school assignment, уникально среди Bay Area городов). Самый diverse housing stock: Victorians в Noe Valley / Pacific Heights / Western Addition, mid-century в Sunset / Richmond, кондо в SOMA / Mission Bay / Hayes Valley. UCSF Mission Bay NICU уровня IV, ZSFG Level I trauma.
