Why Menlo Park

Menlo Park sits between Palo Alto (south) and Atherton / Redwood City (north), with US-101 on the bay-side edge and El Camino Real bisecting the city. Population around 35,000. Meta (formerly Facebook) headquarters anchors the bay-side; Sand Hill Road's venture-capital corridor straddles the Menlo Park / Palo Alto border on the western edge. The K-8 district situation is fragmented: Menlo Park City School District (top 2% California) covers central and east Menlo Park; Las Lomitas Elementary School District (Niche #72 California elementary, #47 California middle) covers west Menlo Park and Atherton; Ravenswood City School District covers a small east-Menlo-Park slice near East Palo Alto. All three K-8 districts feed into Sequoia Union High School District at the 9-12 layer, with Menlo-Atherton High School (U.S. News #229 California) as the primary high school for Menlo Park addresses.

The surrounding Peninsula track record covers the area: 1 Mountain View, 1 San Mateo, 1 Foster City, 1 Belmont, 1 East Palo Alto, each within the same Stanford-anchored hospital catchment as Menlo Park. Menlo Park single-family typically runs $2.5M to $5M+ in the central Menlo Park City SD attendance, $3M to $8M+ in the Las Lomitas SD west-side and Atherton-adjacent areas, and $1.4M to $2.5M in the east-side near US-101. Condos and townhomes run $1.0M to $2.0M. Menlo Park pricing tracks 10 to 20% below Palo Alto per square foot for comparable single-family.

Schools (Menlo Park City SD + Las Lomitas Elementary SD + Sequoia Union HSD)

Menlo Park is one of the most district-fragmented K-8 cities on the Peninsula. Menlo Park City School District (MPCSD, 2,685 students, state rank #33 of 1,908, top 2%) covers central and east Menlo Park. Las Lomitas Elementary School District (Niche #72 California elementary, #47 California middle) covers west Menlo Park and Atherton. Ravenswood City School District covers a small east-Menlo-Park slice. All three K-8 districts feed into Sequoia Union High School District (9,505 students, 10 schools), with Menlo-Atherton High School (U.S. News #229 California) as the primary high school for Menlo Park addresses.

The K-8 district fragmentation is the highest-leverage school variable in Menlo Park buying. The same street can have two different K-8 attendance areas depending on the parcel; MPCSD and Las Lomitas both rank in California's top 5% for K-8, with comparable academic outcomes; Ravenswood is among the most economically challenged K-8 districts on the Peninsula, and the Ravenswood-to-Menlo-Atherton transition is a major step-up. Lily verifies the exact K-8 district by parcel before any offer.

Hospitals and birthing centers

Menlo Park has no in-city hospital with L&D, but the Stanford catchment makes it functionally one of the best-served cities on the Peninsula. Stanford Hospital + Lucile Packard Children's Hospital Stanford (Palo Alto, about 5 to 10 min south) is the de facto in-corridor flagship, with a Level IV NICU. Kaiser members deliver at Kaiser Redwood City (about 10 to 15 min). For north Menlo Park, Mills-Peninsula Burlingame (about 15 to 20 min) is an alternative Sutter PPO option.

Birthing centers: what matters

Lucile Packard Stanford is the default for Menlo Park PPO family buyers: 5 to 10 minutes south, Level IV NICU on-site (the highest available), 24/7 high-risk obstetrics and neonatology coverage.
