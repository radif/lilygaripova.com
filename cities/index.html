Each city has its own pricing arithmetic, school assignment rules, hospital landscape, and Mello-Roos exposure. The pages below are city-specific advisory guides, not boilerplate. Lily walks every prospective buyer through these particulars before any offer is written.

Lily Garipova represents buyers and sellers across the entire San Francisco Bay Area, with 102 documented closings and $111M+ in career volume. The dedicated city-page guides below cover the cities where Lily has the deepest local market record. Pages for additional Bay Area cities are added as Lily's local closing record grows.

Tri-Valley · Alameda County

Dublin, CA

East-vs-West Dublin split, Dublin Ranch / Positano / Schaefer Ranch / Tassajara Hills master-planned tracts, Mello-Roos arithmetic, the Dublin High vs Emerald High decision, BART access on both ends of the city.

2 documented Dublin closings · $1.625M local volume · Dublin Unified School District (#43 in California)

Open the Dublin guide → Tri-Valley · Alameda County

Pleasanton, CA

Downtown Pleasanton historical character, Ruby Hill estate, east-side master-planned (Birdland, Vintage Hills, Sycamore Heights), the 2024-redrawn Foothill vs Amador Valley high school split, Stanford Health Care Tri-Valley in-city.

Tri-Valley specialist across Pleasanton, Dublin, San Ramon, Livermore · Pleasanton Unified School District (#17 in California)

Open the Pleasanton guide → 680 Corridor · Contra Costa County

Walnut Creek, CA

Downtown BART corridor condos, Rossmoor 55+ community, Northgate and Lime Ridge single-family inventory, three-district school split (WCSD K-8 + Acalanes UHSD 9-12 OR Mt Diablo Unified K-12), John Muir Walnut Creek Level III NICU.

4 documented Walnut Creek closings · $5.28M local volume · regional Level II Trauma Center on-city

Open the Walnut Creek guide → East Bay · Alameda County

Fremont, CA

Mission San Jose / Warm Springs vs Centerville / Niles / Irvington vs Ardenwood / Glenmoor, three distinct sub-markets. Apple-Tesla commuter pools, top-California-tier Mission San Jose High, two BART stations.

13 documented Fremont closings · $16.79M local volume · highest single-city closing count of any East Bay city in Lily's file

Open the Fremont guide → Silicon Valley · Santa Clara County

San Jose, CA

Largest Bay Area city, widest inventory variety. Almaden Valley + Cambrian (top schools), Willow Glen (downtown character), Rose Garden / Naglee Park (established prestige), Berryessa / Evergreen (mid-tier). 13+ school districts fragment the city.

24 documented San Jose closings · ~$24.5M local volume · highest single-city closing count in Lily's career

Open the San Jose guide → 680 Corridor · Contra Costa County

Concord, CA

Largest city in Contra Costa County. West Concord (Walnut Creek-adjacent) vs East Concord (Clayton-adjacent). 15-30% price discount to Walnut Creek for comparable inventory. Two BART stations, Mt. Diablo Unified schools.

6 documented Concord closings · ~$5.4M local volume · Mt. Diablo Unified School District

Open the Concord guide → East Bay · Alameda County

Castro Valley, CA

Quiet unincorporated East Bay community, top-rated Castro Valley Unified schools (often outranking Hayward Unified). Hillside inventory (Five Canyons, Greenridge) vs valley-floor SFH around BART. BART access, rare for unincorporated East Bay.

4 documented Castro Valley closings · ~$5.5M local volume · Castro Valley Unified School District

Open the Castro Valley guide → East Bay · Alameda County

Hayward, CA

Second-largest Alameda County city after Oakland. Widest inventory variety, lowest entry price in I-880/I-580 corridor while still offering BART. Hayward Hills + Five Canyons premium hillside vs central walkable BART corridor vs south/Mt. Eden mid-tier.

4 documented Hayward closings · ~$3.2M local volume · 3 BART stations

Open the Hayward guide → Tri-Valley · Contra Costa County

San Ramon, CA

North-Tri-Valley sister city to Dublin / Danville. San Ramon Valley Unified (top California). Windemere + Gale Ranch master-planned (post-2000, high Mello-Roos). Bishop Ranch employer anchor (Chevron, AT&T, PG&E, GE).

0 documented San Ramon closings yet · broader Tri-Valley coverage via 2 Dublin + 1 Pleasanton + 1 Livermore

Open the San Ramon guide → Tri-Valley · Alameda County

Livermore, CA

East-end Tri-Valley city. Wine country lifestyle (Livermore Valley AVA, 50+ wineries 15-25 min east). LLNL / Sandia employer cluster. 15-25% price discount to Pleasanton for comparable inventory; no BART (terminus is Dublin/Pleasanton, 10-15 min drive).

1 documented Livermore closing · Livermore Valley JUSD · Granada High + Livermore High

Open the Livermore guide → Silicon Valley · Santa Clara County

Sunnyvale, CA

Heart of Silicon Valley. South-Sunnyvale (Cupertino Union K-8 schools, $1.8M-$3M+) vs north Sunnyvale (NASA Ames-adjacent, $1.4M-$2M). Apple / Google / LinkedIn / Yahoo employer corridor. May 2026 $3.3M Cheshire Way close.

2 documented Sunnyvale closings · $3.6M local volume · Cupertino Union + Fremont Union HSD

Open the Sunnyvale guide → East Bay · Alameda County

Union City, CA

Between Hayward and Fremont on I-880. Multi-generational Filipino-American and Asian-American buyer pools. New Haven Unified (James Logan High). Single BART station, walkshed premium matters. Priced between Hayward (lower) and Fremont (higher).

5 documented Union City closings · ~$4.9M local volume · New Haven Unified School District

Open the Union City guide → East Bay · Alameda County

Oakland, CA

Most neighborhood-dependent Bay Area market. Rockridge / Temescal / Piedmont Avenue premium ($1.3M-$2M+); Montclair / Crocker Highlands hills ($1.5M-$3M+); flats $700K-$1.2M. Oakland Unified varies dramatically by attendance area. Verify before any offer.

1 documented Oakland closing · broader East Bay coverage via Fremont / Hayward / Castro Valley / Union City record

Open the Oakland guide → Tri-Valley · Contra Costa County

Danville, CA

Tri-Valley corridor between San Ramon and Alamo. San Ramon Valley Unified (top 5% California), Monte Vista High flagship (#93 California), walkable downtown Hartz Avenue, hillside / Blackhawk-adjacent estate inventory.

3 documented Danville closings · $2.51M local volume · San Ramon Valley USD (top 5% California)

Open the Danville guide → East Bay · Alameda County

Newark, CA

Tri-City corner between Fremont and Union City. Newark Unified School District, Bridgeway / Cedar Boulevard master-planned tracts, I-880 + Dumbarton Bridge commute corridor, 10-15% price discount to Fremont for comparable single-family.

1 documented Newark closing · $1.30M local volume · Newark Unified School District

Open the Newark guide → East Bay · Alameda Island

Alameda, CA

Distinctive Bay Area island community. Alameda Unified School District (top 20% California, Alameda High #166 + Encinal #270), East End Victorians vs Bay Farm Island mid-century vs Alameda Point master-planned. Alameda Hospital has no L&D (off-island travel required).

1 documented Alameda closing · $0.60M local volume · Alameda Unified School District

Open the Alameda guide → 680 Corridor · Contra Costa County

Pleasant Hill, CA

Central Contra Costa between Walnut Creek and Concord. Mt. Diablo Unified (College Park High flagship, top 250 California), Acalanes UHSD edge cases on the north slope. Pleasant Hill BART + immediate access to John Muir Walnut Creek Level III NICU.

1 documented Pleasant Hill closing · $1.15M local volume · MDUSD + Acalanes UHSD edge

Open the Pleasant Hill guide → Silicon Valley · Santa Clara County

Los Gatos, CA

South Bay foothills between Saratoga and Campbell. Los Gatos-Saratoga JUHSD (state #27 of 1,907, top 5%; Los Gatos High #89 California). Walkable Santa Cruz Avenue downtown, hillside Aldercroft / Black Road estate inventory, El Camino Hospital Los Gatos in-city L&D (Level II NICU).

3 documented Los Gatos closings · $4.02M local volume · LGSUHSD (state #27, top 5% California)

Open the Los Gatos guide → Silicon Valley · Santa Clara County

Campbell, CA

Central South Bay surrounded by San Jose. Campbell Union HSD (5 high schools ranging Leigh #83 California to Del Mar #574, the school assignment drives a $200K-$500K price spread). Walkable downtown Campbell Avenue, Campbell Farmers Market regional draw.

1 documented Campbell closing · $0.71M local volume · Campbell Union HSD (5 high schools)

Open the Campbell guide →
