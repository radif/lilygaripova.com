What an off-market listing is

An off-market, pocket, or private exclusive listing is a property for sale that never appears on the MLS (the Multiple Listing Service, the shared database agents use to advertise homes) or on public aggregators like Zillow. It moves quietly, within a small circle of agents and qualified buyers.

Why Tiburon sellers choose this route

For many homeowners in Tiburon and Belvedere, privacy is the point. An off-market sale keeps the home off public portals, limits the disruption of open houses and foot traffic, and protects the family's confidentiality. It also lets a seller test a price quietly, without the whole market watching.

How a buyer gets access

Because these homes bypass Zillow and the public portals, there is no listing to browse. The only way in is to contact a local agent who holds the relationships.
