MEET LILY GARIPOVA

Your dedicated real estate professional committed to making your property dreams a reality.

Lily Garipova represents buyers and sellers of residential properties throughout the Bay Area. She sees real estate as financial investment and has a great sense of the local market. She prides herself on guiding her clients on every detail of the transaction with the highest level of honesty and integrity.

Lily believes homebuyers and sellers must be educated before any large commitment. She is a great guide from the beginning to the end of the transaction and beyond. As well as an organizer of online and in-person events addressing many aspects of the Real Estate.

Lily moved to the Bay Area from Nantucket, Massachusetts, where she cofounded a property management company called About Your House, serving high net worth individuals who owned investment properties and exclusive summer residences. Her clients were strictly referral based and chose her for her professional ethics, reliability and experience.

Lily's tech background and highly motivated attitude has been key to her success in real estate. She previously worked as a web developer, and was an active participant of the Woman Who Code Group. She uses her broad tech skills to create extensive online advertising for her listings, and uses her sharp eye for detail to ensure the sellers' property is showcased in the best light possible.

Languages: English, Russian

Memberships: National Association of Realtors® (NAR), California Association of Realtors®, Silicon Valley Association of REALTORS® (SILVAR)
